# Quarterly Learning Performance Summary (Synthetic)

## Overview

This synthetic report summarises certification study performance across teams for
demonstration purposes only. All figures are fabricated and do not represent any
real organisation.

## Headline Metrics

- Average study time across all learners: 21 hours
- Overall pass rate for attempted exams: 68 percent
- Average practice score at time of exam: 76 percent

## Observation: Study Hours and Practice Scores

Learners who logged more than 20 study hours and reached a practice score above
75 percent show meaningfully stronger certification outcomes than learners who
attempted exams earlier with fewer hours logged.

## Observation: Role Patterns

DevOps Engineer and Data Engineer learners showed the most consistent week-over-week
practice score improvement. Security Engineer learners showed the widest variance,
suggesting uneven access to focused study time.

## Recommendation

Teams should track weekly practice score trends alongside hours studied, and flag
learners whose practice score has plateaued below the 75 percent threshold for
additional support.
